Zombie Hallway Escape

Left click to aim (lock cursor). Right click to fire your pistol.

Stay alive, avoid the zombies, and beat the boss at the far end.

Vitals

Health: 100
Boss Health: Alive
Controls
WASD: Move | Space: Hop
Mouse: Look | Left Click: Aim | Right Click: Fire
Stay in the corridor and kite the zombies. Buildings offer cover.
