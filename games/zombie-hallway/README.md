# Zombie Hallway Escape

A lightweight first-person zombie-avoidance demo built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript (Three.js). Battle through a long, football-field-length corridor with scattered buildings for cover while zombies and a boss pursue you.

## How to Run

Open `index.html` in a modern browser. No build steps required.

## Controls

- **WASD**: Move
- **Space**: Hop
- **Mouse look**: Aim your view
- **Left click**: Aim/lock cursor
- **Right click**: Fire pistol

## Gameplay

- Corridor with walls prevents walking through boundaries; buildings provide occasional hiding spots.
- Zombies pursue the player, dealing 10 damage per hit and showing health bars above their heads (80 HP each).
- Player pistol deals 10 damage per shot.
- A boss waits near the end with double size/health and can lob slow fireballs while dealing melee damage.
- Health UI sits in the top-left; boss health is tracked separately.
